FaceRec

Facial Recognition Attendance System

Facial Recognition

The facial recognition technology is used to mark attendance. Live webcam will be started and member's face will be recognized to mark him/her present. Name of the member will be displayed.

Attendance Management

Attendance management includes viewing the records using filters such as date, session, member. Analytics will be provided through graphs & charts. Downloadable Excel sheet also provided of records.

Tip: The attendance records are displayed as numbers
1 - Present    0 - Absent
